About Nepal Tourism

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia situated mainly in the Himalayas. It's known for its beautiful landscapes, diverse culture, and ancient history.

Our mission is to promote Nepal as a tourist destination, showcasing its natural beauty and cultural heritage.
